How It Works

Four steps to a complete estimate

No spreadsheets. No guesswork. Walk through the property and let AI do the heavy lifting.

1

Create Property

Enter the address, property details, and your target rehab level. SnapRehab adjusts everything to your scope.

2

Snap Photos

Follow guided prompts for exterior and each room. The app tells you exactly what to capture for best results.

3

AI Analyzes

Claude Vision identifies defects, flags repair needs, asks smart follow-up questions, and suggests repair items with costs.

4

Get Your Estimate

Receive a complete rehab estimate, scope of work, and export-ready PDF for contractors and lenders.

Features

Everything you need to estimate a rehab

Built by investors, for investors. Every feature exists because we needed it on our own deals.

📷

AI Photo Analysis

Claude Vision and GPT-4o detect water damage, cracks, outdated materials, structural issues, and more from your photos.

💬

Smart Follow-Ups

The AI asks targeted questions based on what it sees. "Does the floor feel spongy near the tub?" "How old is the roof?"

💰

Zip-Code Pricing

Every cost is adjusted to your local market using regional labor and material factors. No more national averages.

📝

Scope of Work

Auto-generated, contractor-ready scope documents. Room by room, item by item. Hand it directly to your GC for bidding.

📈

Detailed Estimates

Categorized line items with quantities, unit costs, and totals. Fully editable. Add your own pricing and adjust anything.

📄

PDF Export

Export professional estimate and scope of work documents. Share with contractors, lenders, and partners instantly.
